TRENCH WARFARE

A WWI Strategy Game

How to Play

  • Draw Trenches: Click and drag to draw trench lines
  • Select Units: Click or drag-select soldiers
  • Move/Attack: Right-click to move or charge enemies
  • Build: Use the toolbar to place emplacements
  • Camera: WASD or arrow keys to pan, scroll to zoom
